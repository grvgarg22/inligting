Recipe Details:

  • Chef:Gaurav Garg
  • Cuisine:North Indian
  • Prep Time:20 min
  • Cook Time:30 min
  • Total Time:50 min
  • calories:450 cal/serving
  • servings:4

ingredients of crispy chicken:

  • 1 kilograms chicken
  • 2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • black pepper as required
  • 2 cup refined oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 eggs- brown
  • salt as required

How to make crispy chicken

Step 1:Coat the chicken with egg and flour

The coating needs to be done with separate ingredients, dedicate one hand to dry coating and one to wet for a lesser mess. To begin, add flour to a deep-bottomed large dish and season it with garlic salt, paprika and black pepper and some regular salt. In a different dish, beat the eggs and add the water to it. Take the washed and dried chicken pieces and dip each piece in the egg mix once and then coat with flour. Repeat this process with each piece and keep them aside.

Step 2:Fry and Serve!

Heat oil in a pan and insert a wooden spoon to check if you do not have a thermometer. If you see lazy bubbles, start adding the chicken and do not move it as soon as you put it into the oil. Let it sit for about 2 minutes. This helps the crumbs stay in place, resulting in a flaky coating. Now move the chicken, take out when a rich golden colour is achieved and serve with your favourite sauce!
